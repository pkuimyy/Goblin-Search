AU Cai Tieshan
TI Elaboration on Further Prosperity and Development of Philosophy Related
SO PROCEEDINGS OF THE 2ND INTERNATIONAL YELLOW RIVER FORUM ON KEEPING
AB Philosophy related social science, like two wheels of a cart and two   wings of a bird as pointed out by President Hu Jintao are as important   as natural science. As such conclusion is also applied to Yellow River   harnessing field, it is necessary to attach great importance to the   development of philosophy related social science in the cause of Yellow   River harnessing to realize the historical task of "keeping healthy life   of the Yellow River".   The wide applications of philosophy related social science to guidelines   of Yellow River harnessing, strategic research, management of Yellow   River harnessing course, social evaluation of projects, treatment of   Yellow River according to law and propaganda work etc. provide strong   support in thoughts, spirits and wits to the development of various   causes. Since the time of harnessing Yellow River, remarkable   achievements have been made in the field of philosophy related social   science by Yellow River Conservancy Commission such as establishment of   the guiding status of Marxist philosophy; studying strategic problems of   Yellow River harnessing with the notion of social science; great   enhancement of legal consciousness of Yellow River harnessing teams;   establishment of media system composed of newspaper, net magazine and   television. Remarkable results have been achieved in the research on   water history. However, what is more wanted for philosophy related   social science of Yellow River Conservancy Commission is shown in the   following aspects: lack of scientific development program; lack of   inspiration mechanism; lack of subject bellwether and lack of propaganda   base. Measures taken for the further prosperity and development of   philosophy related social science are: unalterably using Marxist   philosophy to guide Yellow River practice; establishing subject program;   setting up initiation mechanism; building propaganda base and training a   colony of subject bellwether.   Overall, philosophy related social science is an important part in   Yellow River harnessing cause and if such cause could be developed   quickly, it would hover like having a pair of forceful wings in the sky   in promoting Yellow River harnessing and development and management   modernization.
