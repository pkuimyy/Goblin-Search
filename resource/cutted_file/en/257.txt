AU Meszaros, Ondrej
TI MARXIST PHILOSOPHY IN HUNGARY AFTER 1945
SO FILOZOFIA
AB The paper offers a description of the transformations of Marxist   philosophy in Hungary after 1945 seen from a historical perspective as   well as in the works of their well-known representatives. Among the   philosophers included are: Gy. Lukacs, the representatives of so called   "Budapest school" (A. Heller, F. Feher, M. Vajda), F. 'Tokei with his   "marxology, and E. Ancsels' anthropological project.
