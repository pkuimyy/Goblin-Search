AU Almeida, Felipe Quintao
TI From the linguistic turn to the ontological turn on the epistemological
SO MOVIMENTO
AB This paper reflexes on the interpretation of some Marxist tradition on   the linguistic turn within the epistemological activity in Physical   Education. It describes the suggested reaction by that point of view   (ontological inflection) toward a opposing position against that turning   point. It concludes analyzing some implications of the proposed reaction   for the epistemological activity in physical education.
