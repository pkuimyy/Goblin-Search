AU Zhdan, A. N.
TI AN Leontiev as the leader of the psychological science of the Soviet era
SO VOPROSY PSIKHOLOGII
AB The paper discusses the work of the leader-of the Soviet era psychology   A.N. Leontiev as part of reviewing theoretical heritage of the Soviet   times, reconstructing the history of his life and work. Singling out   different periods in A.N. Leontiev creative career, the author describes   evolution of his philosophy, co-relation of the theory of activity and   contemporary approaches in world psychology. The author concludes that   the theory of Activity is still very much alive and suggests guidelines   for its interpretation and development.
