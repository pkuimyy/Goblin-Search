AU Sivak, Jozef
TI Igor Hrusovsky as a philosopher (In the light of his 'Problemov
SO FILOZOFIA
AB The paper offers a closer view on the main representative of the   philosophical thinking, the founding father of the analytical philosophy   and an innovator of Marxist philosophy in the 1950s and 1960s in   Slovakia, who would have been 100 years old this year. Besides the   account of the problems, with which Hrusovsky has been concerned (such   as being, structure, dialectic) the attention is paid also to their   intellectual background. The author outlines also their further possible   articulation.
