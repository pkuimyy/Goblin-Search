AU Kolganov, A. I.
TI Civilization Approach and White Spots of Marxism: East, West and Market
SO VOPROSY FILOSOFII
AB Author argue, that modern Marxist philosophy of history gives   methodological backgrounds, enough to show social and economic nature,   reasons and consequences of development of so called "eastern" and   "western" civilizations. This creates opportunity to show reasons of so   called "clash of civilizations" and formation of the "center" and   "periphery". Article also shows, that market is not "natural" invariant   of civilization, but historically concrete economic system, which   produce specific for it cultural forms, type of human being and so on.
